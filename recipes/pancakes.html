Breakfast pancakes

Description

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day. But not just any breakfast meal can cut it. You need a great breakfast meal, chocolate pancakes are the perfect breakfast meal. They are easy and quick to make, for sure everyone in the fam will love them.

Ingridients

  • 150g plain flour
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 60g caster sugar
  • 2 large free-range eggs
  • 180ml semi-skimmed milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Chocolate chunks, mini marshmallows and biscuit crumbs to serve (optional; see tips)

For the chocolate sauce

  • 200g Nutella
  • 125ml double cream

Steps

  1. Put the flour, cocoa, baking powder, sugar and a pinch of salt in a large mixing bowl, then whisk to combine. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients, whisk in the eggs, then pour over the milk and whisk again until smooth and combined. Rest the batter in the fridge for 30 minutes (see Make Ahead)
  2. To make the chocolate sauce, gently heat the Nutella and cream in a pan, stirring until melted and smooth. Remove from the heat (see tip). Re-warm to melt again before serving, if needed.
  3. 3 Heat a large frying pan over a medium heat, add a little oil and use a pastry brush or kitchen paper to wipe it across the base of the pan. Scoop small ladlefuls of batter into the pan and cook for about 1 minute on each side. Set aside, covered, to keep warm. Repeat with the remaining batter, adding a little more oil to the pan as needed (see Make Ahead).
  4. Drizzle the chocolate sauce over the pancakes and serve sprinkled with a rocky road topping if you like (see tip).
