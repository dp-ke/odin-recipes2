Fish Stew

Description

A Tilapia fishstew recipe is one of the easiest yet delicious dishes to make on any given night for the entire family. It is simple because it does not require alot of ingridients to make. Every ingridient can be purchased from your local mama mboga market.

It can be served with ugali, chapatti or rice.

ingridients

  • Fresh tilapia full fish
  • 2 white onions
  • 3 tomatoes
  • Corriander leaves(dania)
  • Bell pepper(pilipili hoho)
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Royco cubes(optional)

Steps

  1. In a pot chop you onion and fry for about 2mins then add your garlic fry till golden brown........now add your chopped tomatoes and salt
  2. Let the tomatoes cook well for about 4mins then add some little water for the stew.....let it boil for 2mins
  3. Aside sprinkle lemon drops juice in both sides of your fish to enhance the taste and keep off fish smell......now add your fish to tomato soup....let it boil well for about 6mins to create that stew very well
  4. When done now serve with accompaniment of your choice......I loved it with steamed veges and ugali.......enjoy!!!
